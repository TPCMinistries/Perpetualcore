## 1. Positioning

### 1.1 The product, in one sentence

> "The capture team your organization couldn't afford to hire — discovering the right RFPs, drafting in your voice, ready to submit."

### 1.2 Brand architecture

The Perpetual Core RFP & Proposal Engine is the first commercial product released under The Perpetual Core, the technology company in the broader venture stack. Future Perpetual Core products (the Plaud Voice Agent, the Boardroom Production Agent, the Compliance Filing Agent) will share the same brand house, the same multi-tenant infrastructure, and the same audit-grade philosophy. The RFP & Proposal Engine is the wedge — the product that earns the trust and the customer base that the rest of the Perpetual Core suite will graduate into.

### 1.3 Category

We are not a grant database. We are not a generic AI writer. We sit in a new category: **AI-Native Capture Operations**. The capture function in government contracting and grant-seeking has historically been a $90K–$180K human role staffed only by organizations with $5M+ in annual contract revenue. Below that line, capture is improvised by founders, executive directors, and program staff stealing time from delivery. **That is the gap we close.**

### 1.4 Audiences and what they buy

| Audience | Pain | What they hear from us |
|----------|------|------------------------|
| Workforce dev nonprofits ($500K-$10M) | Miss 70% of fitting RFPs; one writer can't keep up | Never miss the right grant; submit 3x more, win more |
| Faith-based and community CBOs | No proposal infrastructure; intimidated by federal | Federal-grade proposals without a federal-grade team |
| Healthcare and education nonprofits | Generic AI writers don't sound like us | Trained on YOUR wins, in YOUR voice |

### 1.5 What makes this defensible

- **Voice fingerprinting** — your proposals sound like you, not like ChatGPT. Single hardest thing for competitors to copy.
- **Vault-grounded drafting** — every claim cites a real artifact in your vault, with `[VERIFY]` markers for anything else.
- **Multi-entity native** — most competitors assume one nonprofit. We serve consultants, agencies, and dual-mode operators on day one.
- **Dogfooded by Uplift** — every feature gets pressure-tested on real DYCD, DOE, HHS, HRA submissions before we ship externally.
- **Audit-grade** — full agent activity logs for FOIA, OIG, or funder audit. Federal-buyer requirement.

### 5.5 How it works

1. **Upload your wins** — Past proposals, capacity statements, partner letters, 990s, audits. We extract a Capture Profile and voice fingerprint.
2. **We learn your voice** — Signature phrases, register, theory of change language. Quarterly auto-refresh keeps it current.
3. **Discovery runs** — We scan federal, state, city, and foundation sources every six hours. You see only Tier-A matches.
4. **You click Draft** — Pick a section. Paste the solicitation excerpt. The Drafting agent returns a section in your voice, citing your vault.
5. **Reviewer agent grades it** — An Opus-powered reviewer scores against the funder's rubric and flags weak spots before you read it.
6. **Compliance gate** — Page limits, attachments, budget math. Pass before submit.

### 5.7 Why not just use ChatGPT?

Three reasons:
1. **Generic AI writes generic prose.** Funder reviewers can smell it. Voice fingerprinting is what makes our drafts read like you.
2. **Generic AI invents partners and statistics.** Our vault-grounded drafting only cites artifacts you've uploaded, with `[VERIFY]` markers for anything else. Non-negotiable for federal compliance.
3. **Generic AI has no audit trail.** Federal funders increasingly ask how AI was used in your submission. We give you the log they need.

### 5.8 FAQ

- **Will my data be used to train models?** No. Vault content is yours and never enters any model training pipeline. Every prompt and response is logged for your audit, encrypted at rest with per-tenant keys.
- **Can the engine submit on my behalf?** By design, no. Final submission is always human. We get you to submit-ready, not past it.
- **How accurate are the win probability estimates?** Calibrated against your past wins and historical funder data. Guidance, not gospel. As you win and lose with the engine, they sharpen.
- **Do you replace our grant writer?** Most teams find their writer becomes 3-5x more productive. Job changes from writing first drafts to directing the agent and editing for excellence.
- **Is this legal for federal proposals?** Yes. Federal funders increasingly accept and expect AI assistance with proper disclosure. Our audit log produces the documentation you need.
