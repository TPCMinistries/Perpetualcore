## WHY THIS COPY (decision audit)

- **Compresses 7-product portfolio into one buyable thing.** The $75K Engagement is the only SKU a buyer needs to evaluate. Atlas/Sentinel/Sage/Vellum are downstream products that ship later or in adjacent buyer paths. The homepage no longer asks the buyer to choose between products before they've decided to engage.
- **"Tenanted instance of Platform" = the Jenna-equivalent move.** John Morgan's stickiness pattern (named software per engagement) is delivered without naming a new product — Platform already exists, already shipped through the OpenClaw upgrade in February. We just stop hiding it.
- **$10K/mo retainer (not $5–15K range).** Inside the locked $5K–$15K range; anchored at "the same as one mid-level hire," matching John's $15–17K market data with conservative positioning. Single price > range for a homepage CTA.
- **Three slots per quarter.** Real scarcity (you can't credibly run more), creates urgency, justifies the price floor without discounting.
- **"Not in PowerPoint" trust line.** Reuses Position #2 from BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md verbatim. PEPFAR/IRB/GDPR/offline-first language already locked.
- **No client names.** "Faith-based health systems in Brooklyn, parish networks in East Africa, community-college workforce programs in New York" — abstracted only, per locked rule.
