<objective>
Build /products/atlas-discovery as the lower-friction entry point to the Atlas product. It's a productized $5,000–$15,000 audit that fund Operating Partners can buy into without committing to a full Atlas install — pilot-extinction defense per Atlas master spec. The existing /products/atlas page (by-invitation only, no pricing) stays as-is for the flagship motion; Atlas Discovery sits as its sibling for the "I want to see what an Atlas install would look like before I commit" buyer.

URL decision: **`/products/atlas-discovery`** (sibling of `/products/atlas` under the Products portfolio), NOT `/studio/atlas-discovery`. Rationale: the studio site IA per BRAND_ARCHITECTURE §7 places all Atlas-branded surfaces under `/products/`, and the Atlas Discovery audit is a product (it's productized — that's the whole point). Putting it under `/studio/` would imply it's an engagement tier, which it isn't — it's a pre-engagement diagnostic with its own SKU. Consistency with `/products/atlas` matters for buyer mental model.

Purpose: STUDIO-AD-01. Atlas alone reads as too high-friction for funds doing initial AI diligence on portcos. The audit gives them a $5K–$15K SKU to buy as a first step — generates revenue, screens fit, and converts into full Atlas installs for the right portcos.

Output: A live /products/atlas-discovery page reachable from /products/atlas + footer, with a working intake form that lands sales_contacts rows tagged for Atlas Discovery routing.
</objective>
