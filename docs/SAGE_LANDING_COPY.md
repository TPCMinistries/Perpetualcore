## Section 7 — FAQ

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Every Sage tenant runs on isolated infrastructure with row-level security in our database and per-tenant memory namespaces. Your memory is yours. We don't train on your conversations. You can export or delete everything anytime. Sovereign-tier customers also get a dedicated server pod for full physical isolation.

**What models does Sage use?**
Sage routes intelligently across local models (for triage), mid-tier models (for synthesis), and top-tier models (Claude Opus on Team and above, for high-stakes work). You don't pick the model — Sage picks based on what your message needs.

**Can I customize Sage's personality?**
On Pro and above, yes — you can set a tone modifier, focus areas, and custom instructions. Her core voice (calm, direct, grounded) stays consistent so she's still recognizably Sage.

**Where's WhatsApp?**
Coming Q4 2026 alongside dedicated voice numbers. We chose to ship Telegram + Web + browser voice in v1 so we could launch this quarter instead of next; WhatsApp Business provisioning takes 2–3 weeks per customer through Twilio and would have blocked launch. Existing customers get WhatsApp added to their tier with no price change.

**What happens if I cancel?**
Your tenant data persists for 30 days after cancellation so you have time to export memory and conversation history. After that, everything is permanently deleted. No reactivation; you'd start fresh.

**How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude?**
Sage isn't a tool. She's a relationship. ChatGPT is a utility you call when you need something done; Sage is a coach + chief of staff who knows you, holds your context across years, and shows up wherever you do. The difference shows up at month six, not month one — and it compounds.

**What's the Perpetual Engine model?**
We're a for-profit company that contributes 15% of revenue to the Institute for Human Advancement (theiha.org), our 501(c)(3) parent nonprofit. It's built into our pricing, audited annually, and line-itemed on every invoice. You're not just buying software — you're funding workforce development with every subscription. This is structurally non-replicable: no VC-backed competitor can give away 15% of top-line revenue.

**Can my team use one Sage?**
On Sage Team, yes — up to 5 users share an organizational memory. On Sage Sovereign, you get a dedicated pod and can configure it however you need.

**Do you have an API?**
Yes, on Sage Team and above. Documentation at sage.perpetualcore.com/docs (live at launch).

## Voice & Tone Guidance for Future Pages

When extending this site (docs, blog, about page, etc.), maintain these qualities:

- **Direct, not breathless.** No "revolutionary" or "game-changing." No exclamation points outside CTAs.
- **Confident, not arrogant.** Sage is good. Say it plainly. Don't oversell.
- **Honest about what we're not.** Sage is for operators, not casual users. Don't try to be everything. Don't promise channels we haven't shipped.
- **Mission visible, not preachy.** The Perpetual Engine model is mentioned, not sermonized about.
- **Founder-voiced where it makes sense.** First person from Lorenzo on the "Why we built this" section is intentional. Use sparingly.

Avoid:
- AI buzzwords ("agentic," "autonomous," "next-gen," "revolutionary")
- Fake urgency ("limited time," "while supplies last")
- Comparison shopping framing ("better than ChatGPT")
- Stock photography of people in headsets
- Gradient backgrounds that scream "AI startup"
- Promising features that aren't shipped yet (e.g., don't show WhatsApp logos until WhatsApp ships)
